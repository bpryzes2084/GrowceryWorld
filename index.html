Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?

Most verifications are completed within 24 hours. You'll receive an email confirmation once approved.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Annual memberships are non-refundable but remain active until the end of your membership year.

Can my family use my membership?

Yes! All household members at the same delivery address can use your membership benefits.

What if I have multiple qualifying statuses?

You can only hold one membership at a time. Choose the one with the best price for your needs.
